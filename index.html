about me

Growing up seeing and being affected by the social problems around him, Haqi strives to drive the necessary changes to make unimaginable impact so that everyone can live a better life. Haqi is pursuing a bachelor’s degree in Political Science at Airlangga University with GPA 3.71.

During Haqi’s bachelor’s degree, he collects experience in running non-profit and academic projects. Haqi also received academic awards such as being a champion in various debate competitions, participating in various academic events, trying to be an advocate for various academic administrations. With that, Haqi is trusted as Director General of Academic Development at Faculty Student Executive Board and Head of Academic and Social-Political Strategy in Student Association of Political Science at the same time.

Haqi has worked in several public institutions, such as being intern and then was hired as part-time staff in TurunTangan Foundation, media staff for Vice Chairman of Committee for Inter-Parliamentary Cooperation (BKSAP), House of Representatives of the Republic of Indonesia, and most recently becoming an intern staff in 4th largest political party in Jakarta.

Haqi’s main areas of practice are non-profit project management and project marketing, learning and development, public advocacy, CSR and Grantmaking, and Interest Group Studies. He got these organizational achievements and diverse experiences by showing integrity, good performance, and a great sense of responsibility from one experience to one another.
